Site Name: Tooele County Chamber and Tourism

Site name was chosen to match the current name of the website used by the Chamber (and the current site is broken and maybe they'd like a new one?)

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Tooele County Chamber of Commerce is (1) to create a positive climate in which business, tourism and industry can prosper in the general Tooele County Area, (2) to serve as an advocate of the business community in public issues, and in government or legislation which affect the business environment, (3) to advance and cultivate the general progressiveness and civic pride of the community and (4) to promote tourism projects, programs and decisions which enhance the general quality of life of the community and it economic well being. (copied directly from their site)
